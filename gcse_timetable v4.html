⚗️

Science — Tassomai

Do at least 20 Tassomai questions right after your science lesson while it's fresh. Aim for daily streaks — the spaced repetition it uses is highly effective.

📐

Maths — Dr Frost

After each lesson, complete the matching Dr Frost topic. Focus on topics you got wrong — re-doing familiar ones won't help as much.

📖

English

After every lesson, write a short summary of the key points in your own words. Build a bank of quotes for literature texts — test yourself regularly.

🇩🇪

German — Duolingo + Language Nut

Keep your Duolingo streak daily (even just 5 min). Use Language Nut to drill vocabulary sets from your actual GCSE topics like food, travel, and family.

🙏

RE — Oak Academy

Oak Academy has full GCSE RE lessons. Watch one per week and make short bullet-point notes. RE rewards structured essay answers — practice these early.

💡

During PSHE

Since you don't need to actively engage, use these slots for low-noise tasks like Tassomai quizzes, Duolingo, or reviewing flashcards — perfect multitasking.
